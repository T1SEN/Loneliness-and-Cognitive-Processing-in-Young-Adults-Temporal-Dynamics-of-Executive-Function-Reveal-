THEORETICAL IMPLICATIONS
================================================================================

1. GENDER-SPECIFIC COGNITIVE COSTS:
   - Loneliness affects males ONLY (zero effects in females)
   - NOT due to differential loneliness levels
   - NOT due to mood confounding (DASS controlled)
   - Suggests male vulnerability to social isolation

2. STRESS AS MEDIATOR:
   - Stress (not depression/anxiety) is primary pathway
   - Bridge centrality identifies specific mechanism
   - Clinical target: Stress management over mood treatment
   - Aligns with resource depletion theory

3. GENERALIZED DYSFUNCTION:
   - NOT selective to lapses (tau)
   - Affects mean, SD, tau (ENTIRE distribution)
   - Suggests broad resource depletion
   - NOT focal attention failure

4. DUAL-TASK SENSITIVITY:
   - PRP (dual-task) > WCST > Stroop
   - Coordination demands most affected
   - Suggests central bottleneck impairment
   - Aligns with executive control theory

5. INTERMITTENT IMPAIRMENT:
   - Males CAN perform well (mean RT affected modestly)
   - But UNPREDICTABLE lapses (tau, RMSSD)
   - Trial-to-trial inconsistency
   - Real-world consequences (unreliability)
