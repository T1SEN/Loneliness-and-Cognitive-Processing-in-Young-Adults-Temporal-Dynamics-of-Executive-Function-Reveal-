This synthesis analysis provides a comprehensive, multi-method evaluation of the
relationship between UCLA loneliness and executive function (EF) performance,

KEY FINDINGS:
-------------
1. GENDER-SPECIFIC VULNERABILITY CONFIRMED
   - Significant UCLA × Gender interactions for WCST PE (β=2.517, p=0.025) and
     PRP Bottleneck (β=68.802, p=0.036)
   - Male-specific effect: UCLA → WCST PE (β=2.900, p=0.019)

3. NO MAIN EFFECTS AFTER FDR CORRECTION

1. Effect sizes are modest relative to within-group variability

predictors collectively have explanatory power even if individual effects are
marginal.

RECOMMENDATION:

Use continuous predictors (not group splits) for hypothesis testing to preserve
statistical power. Reserve group comparisons for visualization and
clinical interpretation.
