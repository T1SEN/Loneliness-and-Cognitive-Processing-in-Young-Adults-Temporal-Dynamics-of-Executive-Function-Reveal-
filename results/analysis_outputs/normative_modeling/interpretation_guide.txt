NORMATIVE MODELING INTERPRETATION GUIDE
======================================================================

RESEARCH QUESTION:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Does loneliness predict EF impairment BEYOND what is expected
given age, mood (DASS), and gender?

APPROACH:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
Stage 1: Build normative model EF ~ f(Age, DASS, Gender)
Stage 2: Compute personalized deviations (Observed - Expected)
Stage 3: Test Deviation ~ UCLA * Gender

INTERPRETATION:
----------------------------------------------------------------------
- Positive UCLA beta: Loneliness predicts WORSE-than-expected EF
- Positive Interaction beta: Effect stronger in males
- Negative beta: Loneliness predicts BETTER-than-expected EF
- Beta near 0: No UCLA effect beyond demographics/mood
